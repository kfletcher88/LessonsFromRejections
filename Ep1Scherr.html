Dr. Rachel Scherr

Dr. Rachel Scherr is an Assistant Researcher at the University of California, Davis and the Director of the UC Davis Center for Nutrition in Schools. She obtained her Ph.D. in Nutritional Biology from UC Davis and currently focuses her research on nutrition education and promotion in school-aged children. You can read more about her research on her UC Davis Staff webpage.
